AresScan+ – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12 December 2025

AresScan+ (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is developed by Ares Defence Labs Pty Ltd, an Australian company.
We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how AresScan+ handles your information.

⸻

1. Information We Do Not Collect

AresScan+ is designed with privacy in mind.
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

Specifically:
	•	We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
	•	We do not track your location.
	•	We do not access your contacts or calendar.
	•	We do not send any documents, images, or files outside your device.
	•	We do not share any data with third parties.

All processing (including AI, document scanning, or analysis) happens locally on your device.

⸻

2. Information Processed on Your Device

AresScan+ may require access to certain system permissions to function properly.
These are used only on your device and are never uploaded.

Camera Access

Used for:
	•	Document scanning
	•	Real-time detection
	•	Barcode or QR scanning (if applicable)

Images stay on your device unless you choose to save or export them.

Photos / Storage Access

Used for:
	•	Importing files
	•	Saving scanned documents

We do not access any other files, and nothing is transmitted outside your device.

⸻

3. Optional Cloud Features (If You Enable Them)

If you choose to connect a cloud provider (Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox, etc.), files may be uploaded only at your request to your chosen service.

We never receive or store these files.

⸻

4. Analytics

By default, AresScan+ does not collect analytics.

If we enable optional anonymous analytics in the future, we will:
	•	Notify users inside the app
	•	Provide an opt-in prompt
	•	Collect only non-personal, aggregated data (e.g., crash reports)

⸻

5. Third-Party Services

AresScan+ does not use advertising services or third-party tracking SDKs.

If cloud backup or external integrations are used, they follow the privacy policy of the respective provider (e.g., Google Drive, iCloud).

⸻

6. Children’s Privacy

AresScan+ is not directed toward children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

⸻

7. Security

All files processed by the app remain on your device.
You retain full control over your data at all times.

⸻

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.
Any changes will be posted within the app or on our website.

⸻
